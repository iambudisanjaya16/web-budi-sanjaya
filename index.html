About

Design as a way
of thinking.

Budi Sanjaya is a Creative Multidisciplinary based in Jakarta — working at the intersection of graphic design, video production, and creative leadership. Currently pursuing a Master's in Design while actively serving as Multimedia staff at Yayasan Tarumanagara.

With a background spanning visual identity to live event production, each project is approached as a problem-solving exercise — where aesthetics and function are inseparable. Oriented toward a future in design education.

"Desain adalah puzzle yang harus diselesaikan untuk mencapai tujuan. Kehadirannya bukan hanya untuk estetika semata — ada fungsi yang menyertainya."
